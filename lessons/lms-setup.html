LMS ជាអ្វី?

LMS = Website ពិសេស ដែលអ្នក:

  • 📹 Upload Video lessons (structured, protected)
  • 👤 Student enrollment + Progress tracking
  • 💳 Accept payment automatically
  • 📜 Issue certificates upon completion
  • 💬 Community forum / Q&A
Cambodia Context: Online courses selling $29–$199 each ។ 20 students/month × $49 = $980/mo passive income — consistent!

Telegram-Based LMS (Cambodia Favorite)

YOB sees many successful Khmer educators use this system:

1

Create Private Telegram Channel

New Channel → Private → Name: "[Course Name] — Students Only" → Link: generate invite link

2

Host Videos on Google Drive

Upload lesson videos to Drive → Share link → Paste in Telegram (numbered, organized by module)

3

Payment via KHQR / ABA

Generate KHQR payment → Student pays → You add to private channel manually ឬ use Bot

4

Support via Group

Separate Telegram Group for Q&A → Students help each other → Community feeling → Better retention

Launch Checklist

  • ☐ Course outline (Modules + Lessons) finalized
  • ☐ At least 3 lessons recorded (pre-launch)
  • ☐ Sales page / Landing page live
  • ☐ Payment method working (test yourself first)
  • ☐ Welcome message prepared for new students
  • ☐ Beta students (5–10 free) → get testimonials
  • ☐ Launch post → countdown → Launch day CTA
YOB Tip: Launch with minimum 5 lessons ។ Perfect is the enemy of "launched" ។ Students learn from imperfect content — but never learn from content you didn't publish!
