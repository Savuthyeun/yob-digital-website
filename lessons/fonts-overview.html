Font Pairing Guide

Rule of 2: ប្រើ font ២ ប្រភេទ maximum per brand — Display (Heading) + Body ។ More than 2 = confusing design!
  • 🏢 Corporate / Finance: YOB Heritage Display + Kantumruy Pro Body
  • 🛍️ Retail / Fashion: YOB Kantum Bold Display + DM Sans Body
  • 📱 Tech / App: YOB Kantum Display + Inter/DM Sans Body
  • 🎨 Creative / Agency: YOB Display Series + Open Sans Body
