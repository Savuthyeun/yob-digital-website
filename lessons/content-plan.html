YOB 30-Day Content Plan = System ដែល អ្នក ប្រើ grow Facebook Page ពី 0 → Consistent Leads ។ Follow step-by-step, Apply ភ្លាម!

Overview

Goal ៣០ ថ្ងៃ: Build audience trust → Demonstrate expertise → Convert followers to paying customers ។ Result typical: Engagement +200%, Leads +50%

Month structure:

  • Week 1 — Foundation: Who are you + Why trust you
  • Week 2 — Value: Teach + Help audience
  • Week 3 — Social Proof: Results + Testimonials
  • Week 4 — Conversion: Offer + CTA

Week 1 — Foundation Posts

D1

Introduction Post

"ជំរាបសួរ! 👋 ខ្ញុំ [Name] — ម្ចាស់ [Business] ។ [Story + Mission] ។ Follow ប្រសិនបើ Bong/Oun ចង់ [Benefit] !"

D3

Problem Post

"Business ខ្មែរ ៧០% ជួបបញ្ហានេះ..." [Describe pain] → Hook audience who relate

D5

Behind The Scenes

Photo/Video: ការងារប្រចាំថ្ងៃ, Process, Team → Human connection builds trust fast

D7

FAQ Post

"Top 5 Questions ដែលអ្នក DM ខ្ញុំ..." → Shows you listen, positions as expert

Week 2 — Value Posts

D9

Tips Post (3-5-7 format)

"5 Tip [Topic] ជួយ [Audience] [Result]" — Numbered list = Save rate ×3 !

D11

How-To Tutorial

Step-by-step guide ជួយ audience solve 1 specific problem — Video ឬ Carousel

D13

Tool/Resource Recommendation

"Tool ១ ដែលខ្ញុំ Save X Hours/Week..." → Valuable + shareable

D14

Poll/Question Post

"Bong/Oun ជួបបញ្ហា ណាខ្លាំងជាង?" + 2 options → Comment flood + insight on audience

Week 3 — Social Proof

D16

Client Testimonial

Screenshot DM/review + Story = most powerful trust builder ។ "Client [Name] share..."

D18

Before/After Case Study

"[Client] — Before: [Problem] → After [X weeks]: [Result]" → Proof your service works

D21

Numbers Post

"[X months] of [effort] → [Specific results]: +[followers], +[revenue], [ROAS]" — concrete proof

Week 4 — Conversion

D23

Soft Offer

"ចង់ [Result]? DM ខ្ញុំ 'INFO' → ខ្ញុំ share Free [Checklist/Template]"

D25

Limited Offer

"ពេលនេះ YOB ទទួល [X] clients ថ្មី — ចន្លោះ [Date]" → Urgency + Scarcity

D28

Direct CTA

"Ready to [Result]? Link in bio → Free Strategy Call 30 mins — ឥតគិតថ្លៃ !"

D30

Month Recap + Month 2 Preview

Share this month's win + what's coming next month → Retention + anticipation
